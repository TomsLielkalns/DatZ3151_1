Meet Po's Dads

Li Shan

Li Shan is Po's biological father. He is a panda who resides in the secret Panda Village and is deeply proud of Po.

Mr. Ping

Mr. Ping is Po's adoptive father. He is a goose and the owner of the noodle shop where Po grew up. He is loving and supportive of Po's dreams.
